Set up Homework Tracker

Enter your Canvas access token to load your classes and assignments.

Enter just the domain — no https://, no trailing slash.

How to get your token:
1. Log in to Canvas → click your profile picture → Settings
2. Scroll to Approved Integrations → click + New Access Token
3. Name it anything (e.g. "Homework Tracker") → click Generate Token
4. Copy the token and paste it above — it won't be shown again.
